## 11. Risk Mitigation

### Narrative Risks and Counter-Messaging

| Risk | Scenario | Counter-Messaging | Prevention |
|------|----------|-------------------|------------|
| **"Just another safety app"** | Journalists or investors lump Phosra in with parental control apps | "We are infrastructure, not an app. We don't compete with Net Nanny — we make Net Nanny work correctly under Virginia law vs. California law vs. EU DSA." | Lead every pitch with the infrastructure framing. Use the Plaid analogy consistently. |
| **"Too small a raise to matter"** | $950K seen as insignificant vs. Yoti's $210M | "We're pre-seed with 24 months of runway. Yoti raised $210M to build one feature (age verification). We're building the horizontal layer. Capital efficiency is a feature." | Don't lead with the raise amount. Lead with the problem and the product. |
| **"Regulatory risk cuts both ways"** | Laws could be struck down, weakened, or not enforced | "The regulatory trajectory is one-directional. No legislature is repealing child safety laws. COPPA has existed since 1998. The question isn't if — it's how fast." | Cite the breadth (78 laws, 45 states, EU) to show this isn't a single-law bet. |
| **"Pre-revenue / no enterprise customers"** | Challenge to credibility without named platform customers | "50K+ families and 2K+ schools validate the product. Enterprise pilots are the use of funds for this raise. We're selling infrastructure to platforms whose compliance deadline is 50 days away." | Frame current traction honestly. Position enterprise as the funded milestone. |
| **"Privacy concerns about a compliance layer"** | Questions about Phosra's own data handling | "AES-256-GCM encryption. TLS 1.3. Zero-knowledge policy handling. SOC 2 in progress.
