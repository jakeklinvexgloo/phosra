## Pittsburgh Media Events & Opportunities

These are events, programs, and venues where Jake could meet journalists, investors, and community leaders in person.

### Flagship Events

#### Pittsburgh Technology Council Events
- **TechVibe Radio tapings** -- Pitch to be a guest on TechVibe Radio (100.1 FM / AM 1020 KDKA). Contact Jonathan Kersting directly.
- **Face2Face Mixers** -- Periodic networking events at PTC member company locations. Great for casual introductions.
- **New Member Mixers** -- Held at PTC office. Good for establishing Phosra in the PTC community.
- **Think!AI Summit (February 12, 2026)** -- At The Westin Pittsburgh. Executives, data professionals, and innovators. Great venue to network with Pittsburgh tech media and leaders.
- **PTC Annual Events** -- Various throughout the year. Check pghtech.org/events.
- **Business as Usual: PGH Tech Reporters Panel** -- PTC periodically hosts panels with local tech reporters (Alice Crow, etc.). Attend to meet reporters in person.

#### Innovation Works Events
- **IW Venture Expo** -- Annual event at Sheraton Pittsburgh Hotel at Station Square. Brings innovative founders and investors together. Invitation-only for accredited investors and press. A key venue to connect with tech journalists covering the Pittsburgh startup scene.
- **Construction & Robotics Summit** -- Innovation Works convened leaders in November 2025. Shows the range of IW events.
- **General IW events** -- Check innovationworks.org/events for upcoming programming.

#### AlphaLab
- **AlphaLab Cohort Demos/Demo Days** -- AlphaLab selected 20 startups for its 2026 cohort (largest ever). Demo days attract press and investors. Even if Phosra isn't in the cohort, attending demos is great for networking with journalists who cover them.
- **AlphaLab application window** -- Opens annually in spring. Consider applying or attending events as a mentor/judge.

#### 3 Rivers Venture Fair (3RVF)
- **2026 dates: February 4-5, 2026** at David L. Lawrence Convention Center
- Focus on deal-making and industry trends. Select showcase of vetted startups, keynotes, panel discussions, and exclusive meetings.
- Attendees include VCs, founders, and press from across the region.
- Contact: information@3rvf.com / (412) 228-5826

#### CMU Startup Week
- **Inaugural event held September 23-26, 2025.** Multiday celebration of entrepreneurship, innovation, and frontier tech hosted by the Swartz Center for Entrepreneurship.
- Included founder interviews with Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, office hours with VCs, and networking events.
- **2026 dates TBD** -- Watch cmu.edu/swartz-center-for-entrepreneurship for announcements.
