Streaming & parental controls today

Open Banking created unified API standards. Fintechs exploded. Consumers won. The same transformation needs to happen for child safety.

✗

✗

✗

Key Findings

  1. Max has strong baseline controls (dual PIN, maturity tiers) but critical gaps in monitoring and per-title blocking
  2. Content fragmentation across Max, Tubi, YouTube creates a multi-platform compliance tracking challenge
  3. UK launch (Mar 2026) adds Ofcom + OSA + ICO requirements on top of existing COPPA/AVMSD obligations
  4. No third-party parental control app integrates with Max APIs — all operate at device/OS level only
  5. Editorial compliance work (content correctly rated) is undermined when platform controls don't match
